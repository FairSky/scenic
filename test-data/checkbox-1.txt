# Name: Checkbox 1

## Description

There are two checkboxes, labeled "Smart" and respectively "Beautiful".

## Test Sequence

Click the "Smart" checkbox (on the square).
Click the "Beautiful" checkbox (on the square).
Click the "Smart" checkbox (on the text).
Click the "Beautiful" checkbox (on the text).

## Pass/Fail Criteria

The checkboxes toggle their state regardless of the clicks being on
the box or on the text.

Messages printed:

 * "Smart"
 * "Beautiful"
 * "Dumb"
 * "Ugly"
