# Name: Radio Button 1

## Description

There are two radio buttons, labeled "Smart" and respectively "Beautiful".

## Test Sequence

Click the "Smart" radio button (on the square).
Click the "Beautiful" radio button (on the square).
Click the "Smart" radio button (on the text).
Click the "Beautiful" radio button (on the text).
Click the "Beautiful" radio button (anywhere).

## Pass/Fail Criteria

The radio buttons toggle their state regardless of the clicks being on
the box or on the text.
